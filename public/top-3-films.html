Wolfgang's Top 3 Films

Here are my current favorites. What are yours?

The Lord of the Rings

While the books (as usual) are better, these films are incredible adaptations and I could watch them over and over again. Truly remarkable!

No Country For Old Men

This is not a movie I can just play in the background (in contrast to LOTR), but I find the movie to be captivating, and the script is incredible (the opening and final monologues are hauntingly good).

Mud

This is an under-the-radar movie I really, really enjoy. The acting and script feel so authentic in this coming of age story and speaks, I think, specially to the heart of a boy.
